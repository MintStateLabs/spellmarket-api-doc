> The card is expired.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>GENERIC_ERROR</b></code>](#) 
> There was an unknown error with processing the payment.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>INCORRECT_ADDRESS</b></code>](#) 
> The address does not match the card number.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>INCORRECT_CVC</b></code>](#) 
> The CVC does not match the card number.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>INCORRECT_NUMBER</b></code>](#) 
> The card number is incorrect.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>INCORRECT_PIN</b></code>](#) 
> The entered PIN is incorrect.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>INCORRECT_ZIP</b></code>](#) 
> The ZIP or postal code does not match the card number.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>INVALID_AMOUNT</b></code>](#) 
> The amount is either too high or too low for the provider.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>INVALID_COUNTRY</b></code>](#) 
> The payment method is not available in the customer's country.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>INVALID_CVC</b></code>](#) 
> The format of the CVC is incorrect.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>INVALID_EXPIRY_DATE</b></code>](#) 
> The format of the expiry date is incorrect.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>INVALID_NUMBER</b></code>](#) 
> The format of the card number is incorrect.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>PAYMENT_METHOD_UNAVAILABLE</b></code>](#) 
> The payment method is momentarily unavailable.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>WALLET_ON_WRONG_CHAIN</b></code>](#) 
> The wallet on wrong chain to process crypto payments.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>NO_WALLET_DETECTED_CHAIN</b></code>](#) 
> No wallet was connected to process crypto payments.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>PICK_UP_CARD</b></code>](#) 
> The card has been reported as lost or stolen, and the card issuer has requested that the merchant keep the card and call the number on the back.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>PROCESSING_ERROR</b></code>](#) 
> There was an error while processing the payment.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>TEST_MODE_LIVE_CARD</b></code>](#) 
> A real card was used but the gateway was in test mode.
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>OrderTransactionErrorCode.<b>UNSUPPORTED_FEATURE</b></code>](#) 
> The gateway or merchant configuration doesn't support a feature, such as network tokenization.
>
